6 Action Points to Grow Technology Sector Business

A simple, prioritised playbook for the next six months — anchored to Deloitte’s TMT Predictions 2025–2026 research.

Months 1–2
1
Map & Target the Right Clients

Go through your existing tech sector client list and identify where there is untapped potential — who has AI, cyber, or compliance needs you’re not yet serving? Then build a shortlist of 20–30 priority prospects to pursue.

Foundation Pipeline
Months 1–3
2
Lead with AI Governance & EU AI Act

The EU AI Act is the single hottest topic in boardrooms right now. Offer a simple, fixed-price AI readiness diagnostic to tech companies — it gets you in the door, demonstrates expertise, and almost always opens bigger conversations.

AI Act Risk Advisory
Months 2–4
3
Win DORA & Cyber Compliance Work

Technology vendors supplying banks and insurers must comply with DORA. Many are not ready and are under time pressure. Offer a DORA Register of Information review and pair it with a cybersecurity assessment — this is a natural double-sell.

DORA Cybersecurity Regulatory
Months 3–5
4
Sell SOC 2 / ISAE 3402 to SaaS Companies

SaaS companies trying to land enterprise clients are increasingly asked for an independent attestation report. Package a clear, well-priced SOC 2 or ISAE 3402 readiness and attestation offering — it generates recurring annual revenue and builds long-term relationships.

Audit SaaS Recurring Revenue
Months 3–5
5
Run Client Workshops Using Deloitte Research

Use Deloitte’s own TMT Predictions and State of AI in the Enterprise reports as the basis for executive workshops with CTOs, CISOs, and CFOs at tech companies. It positions Deloitte as a thought leader and creates a natural pipeline — clients rarely leave these sessions without a follow-up conversation.

Business Development Thought Leadership
Months 5–6
6
Host a Deloitte TMT Business Forum

In Month 5 or 6, bring together 30–50 senior technology sector leaders for a half-day event anchored around Deloitte’s 2026 TMT Predictions. This creates brand visibility, accelerates existing pipeline conversations, and should generate at least 20 qualified new leads in one session.
